# Al-Rawda Water Production Company – Data Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the operational and sales performance of **Al-Rawda Water Production Company** using Excel and Power BI. The goal of the analysis is to identify production efficiency, sales trends, product profitability, and operational challenges, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance.

## Objectives
- Analyze production trends and product output
- Evaluate revenue and profit performance by product type
- Identify regional and sales channel performance
- Measure operational efficiency and downtime impact
- Detect hidden costs such as damaged bottles and energy consumption

## Tools Used
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Data transformation, modeling, and dashboard visualization  

## Key Insights
- **5 Gallon bottles generate the highest revenue and profit** despite lower production volume.
- Sales are **highly concentrated in Abu Dhabi and Dubai**, creating regional dependency.
- **Production efficiency is high**, with fill rates close to 100%.
- Operational losses occur due to **damaged bottles and production line downtime**.
- Some production lines underperform due to **maintenance and operational inefficiencies**.

## Dashboard Visualizations
The Power BI dashboard includes the following analyses:
- Total Profit, Revenue, and Quality Loss Cost by Product Type
- Revenue by Dispatch Region and Transport Mode
- Monthly Revenue and Units Dispatched
- Revenue and Cost by Sales Channel
- Water Usage Rate by Product Type
- Bottles Produced vs Energy Consumption
- Downtime Impact Cost by Product Type
- Monthly Production, Dispatch, and Damaged Bottles
- Product Efficiency vs Damage Analysis
- Production Line Performance and Sales Contribution

## Key Recommendations
- Increase production focus on **5 Gallon bottles**, the most profitable product.
- Improve **dispatch and logistics efficiency** to convert production into sales.
- Expand **wholesale, subscription, and online channels** to improve margins.
- Reduce **damaged bottles and downtime** to minimize operational losses.
- Diversify sales regions beyond Abu Dhabi and Dubai.

## Outcome
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of production, sales, and operational efficiency, enabling **data-driven decision making** for improving profitability and business growth.
